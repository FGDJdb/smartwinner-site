🔍 Project Name: AIRSS – AI Research & Smart Scraper

🌐 Purpose:

AIRSS is a smart, automated news scraper designed to scan financial news 24/7, identify relevant articles, and prepare them for advanced analysis, summaries, and trend detection via AI (ChatGPT, xAI, Claude and more).

🚀 Features:

  • Realtime web scraping from leading financial sites (using BeautifulSoup + requests)
  • Extracts titles, headlines, and content
  • Prepares structured datasets daily (JSON/CSV)
  • AI-ready pipeline for summary, translation & NLP classification
  • Modular design – can connect to APIs, RSS, Telegram & dashboards

🧠 What makes AIRSS different?

Most scrapers fetch data. AIRSS understands what matters. Even without a paid GPT key, it detects:

  • Breaking economic alerts
  • Relevant market shifts
  • High-value reports
  • Keywords tied to geopolitical or macroeconomic triggers
